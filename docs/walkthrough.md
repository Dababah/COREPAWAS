# Core Pawas - Brand New Multi-Page Website

The Core Pawas digital ecosystem is now live on your local development server. This implementation strictly follows the "Blueprint Lengkap Core Pawas" and expands it into a robust multi-page architecture.

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. The Gateway (Home Page)
Serving as the central hub, the home page features a high-end Hero section with your personal branding photo, a dual-CTA for immediate conversion, and a brief overview of your two core pillars.

### 2. Digital Core (`/digital`)
A dedicated space for your IT services.
- **Service Portfolio**: Highlighting "Pengilon Restaurant" and "Nostra Order System".
- **Workflow**: Transparent 4-step process for client trust.
- **WhatsApp Integration**: High-visibility CTA for consulting deals.

### 3. Financial Hub (`/financial`)
The center for trading and IB XM partnership.
- **Trading Philosophy**: Strategic, logic-based approach to XAU/USD and BTC.
- **Onboarding Path**: A visual 4-step diagram guiding new members from registration to the community.
- **XM & Telegram Links**: Quick access to partner registration and confirmations.

### 4. About & Community (`/about`)
Humanizing the brand with your personal journey as an IT-Trader.
- **Community Values**: Saling Berbagi, Disiplin Tinggi, Pertumbuhan Nyata.
- **FAQ Section**: Proactive answers to build credibility.

### 5. Core Journal (`/journal`)
Your educational hub for sharing knowledge.
- **Market Analysis**: Insights from your trading experience.
- **Tech Tips**: Updates on web development.

## 🎨 Visual Identity
- **Primary Colors**: Deep Navy (`#0A192F`), Aquamarine (`#64FFDA`), Soft Gold (`#F7931A`).
- **Typography**: 
  - Headings: **Plus Jakarta Sans** (Modern & Bold)
  - Body: **Outfit** (Friendly & Premium)
- **Glassmorphism**: High-end translucent cards with subtle glow borders.
- **Live Ticker**: Real-time moving prices for BTC/USD and XAU/USD at the very top.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
- **Framework**: Next.js (App Router)
- **Styling**: Vanilla CSS Modules (Premium flexibility)
- **Logic**: TypeScript
- **State**: Client-side interactivity for Ticker and Animations.
